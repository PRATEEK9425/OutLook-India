AgriTech 2022

Outlook AgriTech Summit & Awards 2022 is a platform for bringing together the top most political leadership, policymakers, thought
leaders and Agricultural Technology companies from India and abroad for sharing the latest knowledge and recognising the best
performers in the country who have made a difference by pushing the envelope using smart technology.

Shri Narendra Singh Tomar, Union Minister of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, is expected to grace the occasion
and encourage select achievers who are doing remarkable work in technological integration in agriculture
sector. The event will be graced with the presence of honorable Chief Minister of Chhattisgarh , Shri Bhupesh Baghel
 .

The event would be accompanied by a campaign supported by experts and researchers working in the fields of
innovation and technology integration in agriculture sector. Outlook will cover the actual picture through
on-ground reporting. The campaign will be supported by experts and researchers working in the fields of
innovation and technology integration in agriculture sector.

Award Categories

  • Best KVK for Digital Innovation
  • ICAR Scientists for Outstanding Contribution
  • Best Agri Institute for value chain management
  • Ministry of Agriculture departments: (UT & State) for outstanding contribution
  • Best Farmers
  • Best FPOs
